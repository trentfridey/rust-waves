Wave Simulator

This is a simulation of the solution of the equation describing the propagation of waves (sound, water, etc): $$\frac{\partial^2 \psi}{\partial t^2} -c^2 \nabla^2 \psi = 0 $$ It runs a finite-difference algorithm written in Rust and compiled to WebAssembly
